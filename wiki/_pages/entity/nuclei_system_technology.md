# Nuclei System Technology

Nuclei System Technology (Chinese: 芯来科技) is a Chinese fabless semiconductor intellectual property (IP) company specializing in the design and licensing of RISC-V processor cores and associated system-on-chip (SoC) platforms. Founded in 2018 and headquartered in mainland China, Nuclei is recognized as a leading RISC-V CPU IP vendor in the country and claims to be the world's first RISC-V CPU IP vendor to achieve ASIL D automotive safety certification for a licensable core. The company's product lineup includes multiple CPU series ranging from low-power embedded cores to high-performance processors, with some cores having passed or fully certified for functional safety levels required in critical automotive subsystems such as drivetrain control. Nuclei's development aligns with China's national strategy to reduce dependence on foreign semiconductor architectures by promoting the open RISC-V instruction set.

## Key Claims

- Nuclei System Technology was established in 2018 as a professional RISC-V processor IP and chip solution company in mainland China.
- The company claims to be the world's first RISC-V CPU IP vendor to achieve ASIL D automotive safety certification for a licensable CPU core (the NA900 series).
- Nuclei offers at least five CPU series; the NA900 has full ASIL D certification, while the NA300 has passed some level of automotive safety certification.
- The company develops high-performance/low-power processors and associated SoC platforms for global computing applications.
- Nuclei's technology is part of China's broader initiative to adopt RISC-V and reduce reliance on foreign semiconductor technology, such as ARM and x86 architectures.
