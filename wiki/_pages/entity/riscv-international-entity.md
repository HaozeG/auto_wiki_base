# RISC-V International

RISC-V International is the non-profit organization that governs the RISC-V instruction set architecture (ISA), an open standard ISA that enables customizable and royalty-free processor designs. The organization facilitates the development and ratification of specifications through technical working groups, supports industries such as automotive and artificial intelligence, holds global summits such as the RISC-V Summit Europe, and offers membership for companies and individuals to influence the evolution of the open ISA. It also provides certification programs, training, and a developer ecosystem to support adoption. The RISC-V Annual Report 2025 highlights the organization's defining role in the semiconductor industry, and the RISC-V Ratified Specifications Library consolidates all ratified specifications into a single repository. RISC-V International also maintains a blog, news announcements, and a calendar of community events, along with resources for developers including hardware and software development guides.

## Key Claims

- RISC-V International is the governing body for the RISC-V ISA specification development, ratification, and maintenance.
- The organization hosts global events such as the RISC-V Summit Europe, held annually to bring together the semiconductor, AI, automotive, HPC, and embedded systems communities.
- It publishes an annual report (e.g., RISC-V Annual Report 2025) documenting the organization's impact and milestones.
- It provides a ratified specifications library as a single source for all approved RISC-V specifications.
- RISC-V International offers membership for companies and individuals, enabling them to influence the ISA direction and access resources.
- The organization provides certification programs, training, and a developer ecosystem to foster software and hardware development on RISC-V.
