# XuanTie C930

The XuanTie C930 is the latest RISC-V-based server central processing unit (CPU) from Alibaba Group, unveiled in March 2025. It is engineered for artificial intelligence (AI) and high-performance computing (HPC) applications, and shipments are scheduled to begin within the same month. The C930 belongs to Alibaba's XuanTie family of processors, which are designed by the company's T-Head semiconductor division. As a RISC-V implementation, the C930 leverages the open-standard instruction set architecture, which offers customization capabilities and does not require licensing fees associated with proprietary architectures such as x86 or ARM. Alibaba intends to release additional RISC-V-based designs following the C930 launch. The chip represents Alibaba's strategy to build competitive domestic server CPUs for cloud computing and AI workloads.

## Key Claims

- Alibaba launched the RISC-V-based XuanTie C930 server CPU in March 2025.
- The processor targets AI and HPC workloads.
- Shipments commenced in the same month of the announcement.
- Alibaba plans to follow up with more RISC-V-based designs.
