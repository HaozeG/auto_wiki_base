# GigaDevice Semiconductor Inc.

GigaDevice Semiconductor Inc. (Chinese: 兆易创新; pinyin: zhàoyì chuàngxīn) is a fabless semiconductor company headquartered in Singapore, specializing in the design and development of Flash memory, 32-bit microcontrollers (MCUs), sensors, and analog products. Founded in April 2005, the company has established itself as a leading supplier of SPI NOR Flash, SPI NAND Flash, and Parallel NAND Flash memory devices, and its GD32 series of ARM architecture-based MCUs are widely used in embedded applications. In June 2026, GigaDevice announced a strategic global partnership with Qt Group Plc, a Finnish software leader in cross-platform design and development tools, to optimize and validate Qt for MCUs on the GD32H7 high-performance microcontroller platform. This collaboration aims to strengthen the embedded graphical user interface (GUI) ecosystem and accelerate next-generation human-machine interface (HMI) solutions. GigaDevice operates as a fabless supplier, relying on third-party foundries for manufacturing, and moved its global headquarters to Singapore in 2025 as part of its international expansion strategy.
