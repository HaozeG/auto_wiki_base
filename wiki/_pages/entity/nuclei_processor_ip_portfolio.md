# Nuclei Processor IP Portfolio

Nuclei System Technology is a Chinese fabless semiconductor intellectual property (IP) company that develops a portfolio of RISC-V processor cores spanning from ultra-low-power microcontrollers to high-performance application processors capable of running Linux. The portfolio is organized into series: the 100 Series (2-stage pipeline, single-issue, comparable to ARM Cortex M0/M0+), 200 Series (2-stage pipeline, single-issue, comparable to ARM Cortex M3/M23), 300 Series (3-stage pipeline, single-issue, comparable to ARM Cortex M33/M4/M4F), 600 Series (6-stage pipeline, single-issue, with DSP and SP/DP FPU, comparable to ARM Cortex A5/A7), 900 Series (9-stage pipeline, dual-issue, comparable to ARM Cortex A35/A53/A55/A9), and additional NX and UX variants that add 64-bit addressing and MMU support for higher-end applications such as data center and baseband processing. Nuclei also offers SoC subsystem IP solutions that integrate pre-verified processor, memory, and peripheral blocks to accelerate customer development. The company has achieved ISO 26262 ASIL-D functional safety certification for its NA900 core, making it one of the first RISC-V IP vendors to meet automotive safety standards. Additionally, Nuclei has collaborated with Quintauris to expand RISC-V solutions into the automotive sector.

## Key Claims

- Nuclei's RISC-V processor IP portfolio is categorized into three classes: N Class (32-bit), NX Class (64-bit), and UX Class (64-bit with MMU), with series from N100 to N900 and corresponding NX/UX variants.
- The N100 series uses a 2-stage pipeline and single-issue execution, comparable to ARM Cortex M0 and M0+.
- The N200 series uses a 2-stage pipeline and single-issue execution, comparable to ARM Cortex M3 and M23.
- The N300 series uses a 3-stage pipeline and single-issue execution, comparable to ARM Cortex M33, M4, and M4F.
- The N600 series uses a 6-stage pipeline and single-issue execution with DSP and single/double-precision floating-point units, comparable to ARM Cortex A5 and A7.
- The N900 series uses a 9-stage pipeline and dual-issue execution, comparable to ARM Cortex A35, A53, A55, and A9.
- The NX600 and NX900 are 64-bit variants of the 600 and 900 series; UX600 and UX900 add an MMU for full operating system support.
- Nuclei offers SoC subsystem IP solutions that integrate processor cores with memory, peripherals, and software drivers to reduce development time and cost.
- The NA900 core achieved ISO 26262 ASIL-D functional safety certification, a first for RISC-V IP (source: search snippet, 28 August 2023).
- Nuclei released the UX1030H processor core with full support for the RVA23 specification (source: search snippet, 29 June 2025).
- Nuclei partnered with Quintauris to develop RISC-V solutions for the automotive sector (source: search snippet, 10 November 2025).
- The portfolio includes security and safety solutions, though specific details are not provided in this resource.
