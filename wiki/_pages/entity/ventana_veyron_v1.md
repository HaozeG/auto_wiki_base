# Ventana Veyron V1

The Ventana Veyron V1 is a high-performance RISC-V processor chiplet designed for data center workloads, announced by Ventana Micro Systems as the first commercially available RISC-V chiplet aimed at the data center segment. Fabricated on a 5 nm semiconductor process, the Veyron V1 features up to 128 custom cores operating at a clock speed of 3.6 GHz, leveraging a scalable chiplet architecture that allows integration of multiple chiplets via a high-speed interconnect. This cluster-based design enables the Veyron V1 to be deployed not only in data centers but also in automotive, 5G infrastructure, artificial intelligence, and client computing applications, marking a significant milestone in the RISC-V ecosystem's expansion into high-performance computing domains previously dominated by x86 and ARM architectures.

## Key Claims

- Ventana is the first company to offer a RISC-V chiplet design targeting data center workloads.
- The Veyron V1 supports up to 128 cores with a maximum clock speed of 3.6 GHz.
- The chiplet is manufactured on a 5 nm semiconductor process node.
- The architecture uses a scalable cluster-based design that allows chiplets to be tiled together for higher core counts.
- Applications span data centers, automotive systems, 5G infrastructure, AI accelerators, and client devices.
