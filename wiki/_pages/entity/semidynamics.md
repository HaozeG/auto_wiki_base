# Semidynamics

Semidynamics is a Barcelona-based European company that specializes in the design of fully customizable 64-bit RISC-V IP cores for machine learning, artificial intelligence, high-performance computing, and data-intensive workloads. Founded in 2016, the company emphasizes a memory-first design philosophy and is developing the world's first terabyte-scale AI processing unit (AIPU) with deterministic low-latency LLM performance, targeting zero-bottleneck, high-density workflows. Semidynamics completed a 3-nm chip tape-out as of early 2026 and is expanding into a full-stack AI infrastructure platform that encompasses chips, boards, and rack-level systems designed for data-center-scale inference deployments. The company aims to support European AI sovereignty by advancing open-standard RISC-V silicon.

## Key Claims

- Semidynamics claims to have developed the world's first terabyte-scale AI processing unit (AIPU) with deterministic low-latency performance for LLM workloads.
- They describe themselves as the European leader in high-performance RISC-V, customizing AI architectures with a memory-first approach.
- The company completed a 3-nm chip tape-out (reported Feb 2026), making it 3-nm ready.
- Semidynamics is expanding from IP cores to full-stack AI infrastructure, including chips, boards, and rack-level systems for datacenter inference.
- They secured a strategic investment to advance memory technology and full-stack platform development.
- The company's cores are fully customizable 64-bit RISC-V, tailored for ML, AI, HPC, and data-intensive workloads.
- Semidynamics' platform is designed for data-center-scale inference deployments, promoting AI sovereignty in Europe.
