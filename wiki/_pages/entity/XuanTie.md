# XuanTie

XuanTie is a RISC-V processor brand under the Alibaba DAMO Academy, established to drive international leadership in the RISC-V ecosystem by leveraging Alibaba's strengths in cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and big data. Since its founding, the XuanTie team has continuously engaged in RISC-V technology research and ecosystem development, releasing a series of XuanTie processors that cover high, medium, and low performance requirements for diverse application scenarios. The brand actively embraces open source and open innovation, gradually building an ecosystem centered around RISC-V in collaboration with partners, including RISC-V chips, development tools, operating systems, and more. XuanTie aims to provide powerful, intelligent, secure, and open new computing architectures and reliable IP cores for the digital era.
