# RISC-V Summit Europe 2025

The RISC-V Summit Europe 2025 is the premier European conference dedicated to the RISC-V open standard instruction set architecture (ISA), scheduled for May 12–15, 2025, in Paris, France. Organized by the RISC-V community, the summit aims to connect industry leaders, government representatives, researchers, academics, and ecosystem partners who are building the future of innovation on RISC-V. With approximately one-third of RISC-V’s global community based in Europe, the event is designed to highlight both commercial and research applications of the ISA. The program includes plenary talks, panels, demos, posters, technical work groups (TWGs), tutorials, an expo area, and social events. The summit also features calls for proposals covering the main program, a board dev zone, a university demo day, and side events, all of which were closed by the time of the announcement. The event serves as a key gathering for the European RISC-V ecosystem to share progress, foster collaboration, and discuss future directions for open computing.
