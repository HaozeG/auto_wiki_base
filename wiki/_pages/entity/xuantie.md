# XuanTie

XuanTie is a family of RISC-V processors developed by T-HEAD (Alibaba Group). According to a Boardor blog post, XuanTie has announced new products including C908X, R908A, and XL200, targeting AI acceleration, automotive applications, and high-speed interconnects. The post states that the high-performance era of RISC-V has arrived and that XuanTie is leading this development with technological innovation and ecosystem construction. The market penetration of RISC-V-based SoCs is projected to increase from 5.9% to 25.7% from 2024 to 2031, indicating the growing adoption of RISC-V architectures across various sectors.
