# SiFive Automotive Family

The SiFive Automotive Family is a portfolio of RISC-V processor cores from SiFive designed specifically for automotive applications. It includes a broad range of optimized safety processors that have received certification to ISO 26262 ASIL B, ASIL D, and split-lock configurations, covering applications such as advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS), autonomous driving (AD), in-vehicle infotainment (IVI), body electronics, powertrain, and central compute domains. SiFive has also achieved ISO/SAE 21434:2021 product certification for cybersecurity. Current announced products within the family include the Automotive E6-A and S7-A cores, and the company has outlined a long-term roadmap for future automotive solutions. The family competes with other RISC-V core families targeting safety-critical and high-performance automotive use cases.

## Key Claims

- Broadest range of optimized RISC-V safety processors for automotive, certified to ISO 26262 ASIL B, ASIL D, and split-lock configurations.
- Targets ADAS, AD, IVI, body, powertrain, and central compute workloads.
- Achieved ISO/SAE 21434:2021 product certification for cybersecurity.
- Current products include Automotive E6-A and S7-A cores.
- Long-term roadmap for automotive SiFive cores.
