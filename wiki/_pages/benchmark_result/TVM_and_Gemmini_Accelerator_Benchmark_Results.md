# TVM and Gemmini Accelerator Benchmark Results

This page documents benchmark results for the integration of the code generation framework TVM with the systolic array-based Gemmini accelerator, as presented in arXiv:2212.03034. The integration enables offloading GEMM (General Matrix Multiply) tensor operations to Gemmini via a generic schedule and leverages AutoTVM for automatic performance tuning. Measurements were conducted on a Xilinx ZCU102 FPGA at a 100 MHz clock, achieving a peak throughput of 46 giga-operations per second (GOPs). The results outperform previous work and surpass the default hand-tuned schedules provided by Gemmini developers on real-world workloads.

## Key Claims

- Integration of TVM and Gemmini enables automatic offloading of GEMM operations.
- A generic schedule for GEMM on Gemmini is presented and tested with AutoTVM tuning.
- Peak throughput of 46 GOPs at 100 MHz on Xilinx ZCU102 FPGA.
- Outperforms previous work and hand-tuned schedules in real-world workloads.
