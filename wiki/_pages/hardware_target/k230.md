# K230

The K230 is an end-side AIoT (Artificial Intelligence of Things) system-on-chip designed by Kendryte, built on the RISC-V architecture. It integrates a dual-core processor subsystem: a high-performance RISC-V core clocked at 1.6GHz with a 128-bit RVV 1.0 vector extension, 32KB I-cache, 32KB D-cache, and 256KB L2 cache, and a secondary RISC-V core at 0.8GHz with matching 32KB I-cache, 32KB D-cache, and 128KB L2 cache. The chip includes a neural network processor (KPU) supporting INT8 and INT16 quantized inference, a 3D structured light depth engine (DPU) with up to 1920×1080 resolution, a video processor (VPU) for H.264/H.265 encode/decode (encoder up to 3840×2160@20fps, decoder up to 3840×2160@40fps), and a JPEG codec supporting up to 8192×8192 resolution. It supports MIPI CSI (up to three lanes) for camera input and MIPI DSI output up to 1920×1080@60fps. On-chip peripherals include 5 UART, 5 I2C, 6 PWM, 64 general-purpose GPIO plus 8 PMU GPIO, 2 USB 2.0 OTG, 2 SD/eMMC 5.0 interfaces, 1 OSPI and 2 QSPI, as well as WDT, RTC, and timer. The K230 targets edge-side large model multimodal access, 3D structured light depth perception, interactive robots, open-source hardware, smart manufacturing, smart homes, and smart education. It provides multiple software development kits including MicroPython, RT-Smart, Linux, and a combined Linux+RT-Smart dual-core environment.

## Key Claims

- Typical KPU inference performance: ResNet50 ≥ 85 fps @INT8; MobileNet_v2 ≥ 670 fps @INT8; YoloV5S ≥ 38 fps @INT8 (source: product page, evidence strength: marketing).
- VPU encoder performance: 3840×2160@20fps; decoder performance: 3840×2160@40fps.
- JPEG codec supports up to 8192×8192 resolution.
- DPU supports 3D structured light depth processing at maximum 1920×1080.
- CPU1 runs at 1.6GHz with 256KB L2 cache and RVV 1.0 128-bit vector extension.
- CPU0 runs at 0.8GHz with 128KB L2 cache.
- Claimed reasoning capability under typical networks is 13.7 times that of the preceding K210 SoC.
- On-chip interfaces include 5 UART, 5 I2C, 6 PWM, 64+8 GPIO, 2 USB 2.0 OTG, 2 SD/eMMC 5.0, 1 OSPI + 2 QSPI, WDT/RTC/Timer.

## Optimization-Relevant Details

- ISA/profile: RISC-V with RVV 1.0 vector extension (128-bit) on the high-performance core.
- Vector/matrix/accelerator support: KPU for neural network inference (INT8/INT16); DPU for 3D depth processing; VPU for video codec.
- Memory/cache/TLB/DMA: Per-core L1 I/D-cache 32KB each; L2 cache 256KB (CPU1) and 128KB (CPU0). No details on TLB or DMA.
- Compiler/toolchain support: MicroPython SDK, RT-Smart SDK (C/C++), Linux SDK (C/C++), Linux+RT-Smart dual-core SDK. Specific toolchain versions not provided in the source.
