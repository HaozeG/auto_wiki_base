# ET-SoC-1

The ET-SoC-1 (Esperanto Technologies Supercomputer-on-Chip 1) is a RISC-V AI inference accelerator chip targeting the hyperscaler datacenter market. Fabricated on TSMC's 7nm process, the chip integrates over a thousand RISC-V processors on a single die, delivering a massively parallel, flexible architecture designed for ultra-low power consumption. It is the first product in Esperanto's family of AI accelerators and is optimized for artificial intelligence and machine learning inference workloads, with a focus on recommendation systems and other datacenter inference tasks. The chip aims to be the world's most efficient commercial RISC-V chip, combining general-purpose RISC-V cores with specialized tensor processors to accelerate ML workloads.

## Key Claims

- Integrates over 1000 RISC-V cores specifically optimized for AI/ML inference.
- Fabricated on TSMC 7nm process.
- Targets hyperscaler datacenter inference market.
- Claims to be the world's most efficient commercial RISC-V chip for inference.
- Ultra-low power consumption design for high efficiency.
- Massively parallel, flexible architecture.
- First product from the Esperanto Technologies AI accelerator family.
